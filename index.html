About Me

I am Silvia Bolzani, PhD, a freelance Medical Writer with a solid background in academic research, particularly in biomedical studies related to immunology and epigenetics. I am passionate about translating complex scientific information into clear, accurate, and engaging content. I collaborate with clients across academia, industry, and medical communications agencies, supporting the development of materials tailored to healthcare professionals, researchers, and patient communities. Whether the goal is to inform, publish, or share science more broadly, I aim to make each message accessible, accurate, and meaningful. I believe that effective science communication can make a real difference for individuals, professionals, and society as a whole.
