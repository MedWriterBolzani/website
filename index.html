Freelance Medical Writer - Silvia Bolzani, PhD

I am Silvia Bolzani, PhD, a freelance Medical Writer with a solid background in academic research, particularly in biomedical studies related to immunology and epigenetics. I am passionate about translating complex scientific information into clear, accurate, and engaging content. I collaborate with clients across academia, industry, and medical communications agencies, supporting the development of materials tailored to healthcare professionals, researchers, and patient communities. I believe that effective science communication can make a real difference for individuals, professionals, and society as a whole.
